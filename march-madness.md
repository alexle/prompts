# March Madness Strategy Checklist (2026 Edition)

## 1. Champion Criteria

- **Seed:** 1 seed or elite 2 seed only.
- **Health:** No major injuries to starting rotation (e.g., Duke's 2026 injuries to Caleb Foster and Patrick Ngongba are red flags).
- **Efficiency:** Must rank in KenPom Top 10 in both Adjusted Offensive and Defensive Efficiency. Cross-reference with ESPN BPI Top 10 for convergence. Discount teams with high KenPom but weak strength of schedule. Avoid teams with over-reliance on 3-point shooting (>45% of scoring).
- **Guards:** Elite backcourt play; must rank Top 25 nationally in Turnover Percentage.

## 2. Final Four Architecture

- **The 3+1 Rule:** Three teams must be 1-2 seeds; one team can be a value pick (3-4 seed).
- **Stability:** Zero teams seeded 5 or higher should reach this stage.
- **Defense:** At least two Final Four teams must rank in the KenPom Top 10 for Defensive Efficiency.

## 3. Upset Allocation (Round of 64)

- **10-Seeds:** Pick 1-2 total. Target "Power Conference" teams with a rough mid-season but high analytical ceilings (e.g., 2026 Texas A&M/Missouri).
- **11-Seeds:** Pick 1-2 total. Maximum of one advances to the Sweet 16.
- **12-Seeds:** Pick 1-2 total. Use as "Chaos Early" picks only; none go past the Round of 32.
- **13-Seeds:** Pick 0-1 total. Only if the 4-seed is outside the Top 50 in Defensive Efficiency.
- **Hard Limits:** No 14-16 seeds advancing. No more than two double-digit seeds in the Sweet 16.

## 4. Round-by-Round Filters

- **Round of 64:** 3-5 total upsets. Focus exclusively on 10s, 11s, and 12s.
- **Round of 32:** Majority of favorites advance. Flush out the 11/12 seeds here.
- **Sweet 16:** All teams must rank in the KenPom Top 30 overall. Cross-check: all should appear in ESPN BPI Top 30.
- **Elite 8 Floor (HARD CAP):** No team outside the KenPom Top 40 in Defensive Efficiency advances past the Sweet 16 (e.g., 2026 Alabama at #68 and Wisconsin at #62 are capped here). When KenPom defensive data is unavailable, use ESPN BPI defensive rating as a proxy — teams with BPI Def below 8.0 are suspect.

## 5. The Cinderella Filter (Max 1 Team)

- **Metrics:** Must rank in the KenPom Top 75 overall and Top 50 in Defense.
- **Ball Security:** Must rank in the National Top 50 for Turnover Percentage ("They don't beat themselves").
- **Personnel:** Veteran guards with elite assist-to-turnover ratios. Lean into NBA-level shot creators.
- **Ceiling:** Sweet 16 is the absolute limit.

## 6. Final Submission Audit

- **Emotion Check:** Remove teams you "like" if they fail the defensive floor rule.
- **Injury Check:** Downgrade any team with a primary ball-handler out (e.g., 2026 Louisville without Mikel Brown Jr.).
- **Balance:** Early rounds = Chaos; Late rounds = Stability.

---

**ONE-LINE RULE:** Pick chaos in the 64, but only advance elite, balanced teams with Top-30 defense, strong guard play, and late-game shot creation past the Sweet 16.

---

## 7. Bracket Scorecard

Use this scorecard to audit a completed bracket against the checklist. Grade each section, flag violations, and produce a final letter grade.

### Section Grades

| Section | Criteria | Grade Scale |
|---------|----------|-------------|
| **Champion** | Seed (1 or elite 2), health (no key injuries), KenPom Top 10 AdjOE + AdjDE, BPI Top 10 convergence, elite guard play | A+ = all pass, B = one soft fail, C = hard fail on efficiency or health |
| **Final Four** | 3+1 seed rule, no 5+ seeds, 2+ teams KenPom Top 10 AdjDE, BPI Def alignment | A = all pass + value pick used, A- = all 1-2 seeds (too chalky), B = one violation |
| **Upset Allocation** | 10s (1-2), 11s (1-2), 12s (1-2), 13s (0-1), no 14-16, total 3-5, max 2 DD in S16 | A = all in range, B = one line over by 1, C = multiple violations |
| **Round Filters** | R64 upset count, R32 flush, S16 all KenPom Top 30, E8 AdjDE Top 40 hard cap | A = all pass, B = one borderline, C = hard cap violation |
| **Cinderella** | Max 1 team, KenPom Top 75, Top 50 Def, Top 50 TO%, S16 ceiling | A = clean or N/A, C = Cinderella past S16 |
| **Pool Strategy** | Champion matches pool size, differentiation for large pools | A = optimized for pool, B = generic/chalk |
| **Audit** | Emotion check, injury check, early chaos + late stability balance | A = no contradictions, B = one flag, C = picks contradict own rules |

### Data Sources (in priority order)

1. **KenPom** (kenpom.com) — primary source for AdjOE, AdjDE, overall rank, tempo
2. **ESPN BPI** (espn.com/mens-college-basketball/bpi) — cross-reference for offensive/defensive ratings; use when KenPom is unavailable
3. **Bart Torvik** (barttorvik.com) — supplementary efficiency and turnover data
4. **Sports Reference** (sports-reference.com/cbb) — SRS, ORtg, DRtg for historical comparison

### How to Score

1. Grade each of the 7 sections independently (A+ through F)
2. Flag any **hard cap violations** (E8 defensive floor, 14-16 seeds advancing) as automatic C or below
3. Flag any **internal contradictions** (e.g., picking a team your own checklist names as a red flag)
4. Compute final grade: weighted average where Champion (2x), Final Four (2x), and Round Filters (1.5x) carry extra weight
